3. The Indian Express (Balanced & Issue-Oriented)
India’s LPG consumption trends reflect a dual reality—expanded access but uneven usage. The rollout of the Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana has been instrumental in increasing coverage.
Yet, recent surveys highlight a decline in refill frequency among economically weaker sections. Analysts point out that the absence of consistent subsidies has impacted consumption behavior.
The focus is now shifting from connection-based metrics to sustained adoption and affordability.
