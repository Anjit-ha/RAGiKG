6. The Tribune (Regional + Social Impact Focus)
The expansion of LPG connections across India has brought significant social and environmental benefits. Rural households, in particular, have benefited from reduced indoor air pollution.
However, the high cost of LPG cylinders remains a concern for many families. In some areas, usage has declined as households try to manage expenses.
Authorities are being urged to introduce more supportive measures to ensure that LPG remains a viable option for all.
