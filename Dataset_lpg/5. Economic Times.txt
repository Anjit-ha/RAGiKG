5. Economic Times (Business & Data-Oriented)
India’s LPG consumption has shown robust long-term growth, supported by policy interventions and rising household penetration. Oil marketing companies have reported consistent increases in domestic LPG demand over the years.
However, recent trends indicate a moderation in growth due to price sensitivity among consumers. The government’s subsidy rationalization has impacted demand elasticity.
Going forward, market stability will depend on balancing fiscal constraints with consumer affordability, especially in rural markets.
