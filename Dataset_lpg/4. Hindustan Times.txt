4. Hindustan Times (Urban + Human Angle)
For many families across India, LPG has transformed daily life by offering a cleaner and safer alternative to traditional fuels. Government efforts have ensured that even remote households now have access to LPG connections.
Still, rising prices are making it difficult for some families to use LPG regularly. In several regions, people are reverting to firewood for cost reasons.
Experts stress the need for continued support to ensure that the benefits of LPG reach every household consistently.
