2. The Times of India (General Public Interest)
India is seeing a steady rise in LPG usage as more households shift to cleaner cooking fuels. Thanks to schemes like Ujjwala Yojana, millions of families now have LPG connections.
Despite this, many households are struggling with rising cylinder costs, leading to reduced usage in some areas. Experts say that while LPG penetration is high, ensuring regular usage remains a challenge.
The government is working on measures to balance affordability and environmental benefits.
